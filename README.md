## Clone the project

1. Open *GitKraken*
1. Hit *Clone a Repo*
1. Go to *Clone* > *GitHub.com*
1. Under *Repository to clone*, enter `FebGameJam` then hit *Clone the repo!*
1. Open *Unity Hub*
1. Go to *Projects* > *Add project from disk*
1. Navigate to the cloned project folder from GitKraken
1. Open the project
